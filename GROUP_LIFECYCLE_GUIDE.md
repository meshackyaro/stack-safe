## FAQ

**Q: Can I reopen a closed group?**
A: No, once closed, a group cannot be reopened. This ensures fairness for all members.

**Q: What if the creator never starts the savings period?**
A: The group remains in "Closed" status. Members can still deposit, but there's no lock period.

**Q: Can I leave a group before it starts?**
A: Currently, once you join, you're committed. Choose groups carefully!

**Q: What happens if I deposit after the group is closed but before it starts?**
A: Your deposit is accepted, and it will be locked once the creator starts the savings period.

**Q: Can the creator change the lock duration after creating the group?**
A: No, the lock duration is set at creation and cannot be changed.
